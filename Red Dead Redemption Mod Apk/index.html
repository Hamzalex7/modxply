About this game

Red Dead Redemption NETFLIX is the mobile port of the legendary open-world Western epic from Rockstar Games. Play as former outlaw John Marston, forced to hunt down his old gang to save his family from federal agents. Explore the vast American West and Mexico, engage in intense shootouts, horseback riding, hunting, and deep storytelling with moral choices that affect your honor system.

This Netflix edition includes the full single-player campaign, all DLCs like the iconic "Undead Nightmare" zombie expansion, and bonus content from the Game of the Year Edition. Optimized for touch controls on mobile, no ads, no extra purchases – just pure classic gameplay with a Netflix subscription.
